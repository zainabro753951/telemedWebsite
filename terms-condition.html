Privacy Policy for TelemedLine

TelemedLine.com (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our telehealth services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, date of birth, address, phone number, email address.

Health Information: Medical history, symptoms, diagnoses, prescriptions, and other healthcare-related information.

Payment Details: Credit/debit card information for billing purposes (processed securely via Stripe).

How We Use Your Information

Provide and manage telehealth services.

Schedule appointments and conduct consultations.

Maintain accurate medical records.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Licensed healthcare professionals providing your care.

Third-party service providers (e.g., doxy.me for video visits, Stripe for payments).

Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards including encryption and secure servers to protect your personal and health information.

Your Rights

Access your personal data.

Correct or update information.

Request deletion (subject to legal requirements).

Withdraw consent for non-essential data use.
